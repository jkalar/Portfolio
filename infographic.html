Infographic Example

This is an example of a longform inforgraphic that i created for a lab assignment while taking GEOG 572 (Graphic Design in Cartography) at the University of Wisconsin. I based my infographic on excise taxes placed on a pack of cigarettes by federal, state, and local governments. I wanted to show if there was a correlation between high cigarette prices due to high taxes rates and smoking rates. I chose this topic as the high price of cigarettes was largely responsible for me quitting smoking.
